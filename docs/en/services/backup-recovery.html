Backup & Recovery

Complete protection for your critical data and guaranteed continuity against any contingency

Enterprise data protection

We implement robust backup and disaster recovery solutions that guarantee protection and availability of your critical data. Our strategies combine leading technologies like Azure Backup, AWS Backup, Veeam and Commvault to create multi-layer backup systems that adapt to your RPO (Recovery Point Objective) and RTO (Recovery Time Objective) goals.

From automated backups to complete disaster recovery plans with geographic replication, we design and implement solutions that protect your information against hardware failures, human errors, ransomware attacks and natural disasters. We perform periodic recovery tests to ensure your data can be restored when needed.

Our protection solutions

Automated Backup

Scheduled backups with configurable retention, deduplication and compression. Protection for VMs, databases, files and applications.

Disaster Recovery

DR plans with replication to secondary sites. Automated failover and failback. RTO and RPO defined by business criticality.

Anti-Ransomware Protection

Air-gapped immutable backups, anomaly detection and rapid recovery from attacks. Offline copies and separate cloud.

Testing & Validation

Periodic recovery tests, backup integrity reports and disaster recovery drills. Runbook documentation.
