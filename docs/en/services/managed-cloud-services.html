Managed Cloud Services

Complete lifecycle management for your Cloud infrastructure from design to continuous optimization

Comprehensive Cloud platform management

Our Managed Cloud Services allow you to focus on your business while we handle all operations, maintenance and optimization of your Azure, AWS or hybrid infrastructure. We manage the complete lifecycle from planning and design to implementation, monitoring and continuous improvement.

We work with ITIL v3 methodology, guaranteeing maximum availability, security and performance. Our team of certified specialists acts as an extension of your IT area, providing level 2 and 3 support, incident management, changes and configurations.

What's included in our managed services?

Complete Administration

Infrastructure, servers, databases, networks and cloud applications management. Configuration, patching and system updates.

Continuous Optimization

Performance analysis, resource rightsizing, cost optimization and improvement recommendations based on best practices.

Specialized Technical Support

Level 2 and 3 support with Azure and AWS certified engineers. ITIL-based ticket, incident and change management.

Change Management

Controlled implementation of changes, updates and improvements. Maintenance planning and change windows.
