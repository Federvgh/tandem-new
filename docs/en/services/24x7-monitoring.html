24×7 Monitoring

Proactive infrastructure monitoring with real-time alerts and immediate response

Continuous surveillance of your infrastructure

Our 24×7 monitoring service provides complete visibility of your IT infrastructure, applications and services. Using industry-leading tools and proactive methodologies, we detect and resolve issues before they impact your users or business operations.

We monitor critical metrics such as CPU, memory, disk, network, application performance, service availability and user experience. Our NOC team receives real-time alerts and responds immediately to any anomaly, following defined escalation procedures and SLAs.

What we monitor

Proactive Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of servers, VMs, containers, applications and network devices. Detection of anomalies before they become incidents.

Immediate Response

Automated alerts to our NOC with defined escalation procedures. Response within 15 minutes for critical incidents.

Detailed Reports

Weekly and monthly reports with performance metrics, incidents, trend analysis and optimization recommendations.

Leading Tools

Azure Monitor, AWS CloudWatch, Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana and PRTG. Unified dashboards for complete visibility.
